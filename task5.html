Getting Started with Client Ramp

Find answers to common questions about setting up and using Client Ramp for your business.

Getting Started

How do I create a Client Ramp account?

To create a Client Ramp account, visit our signup page and enter your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email and complete setup in under 5 minutes.

What information do I need to get started?

You'll need basic company details like name, industry, and number of clients. More details can be added later as you customize workflows.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, a 14-day free trial with all features is available — no credit card required. After that, you can upgrade or pause your account.

Account Setup

How do I customize my onboarding workflow?

Go to the Workflows section in your dashboard to build onboarding steps, set automation, and personalize templates using drag-and-drop.

Can I import existing client data?

Yes, you can import via CSV or integrate with CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot for automatic sync.

How do I add team members?

Admins can invite team members from Settings > Team Management and assign them specific roles.

Billing & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans — all payments are PCI-compliant.

Can I change my plan later?

You can upgrade anytime (features unlock instantly) or downgrade (applies next billing cycle).
